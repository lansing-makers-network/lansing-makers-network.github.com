Lansing Makers Network

Lansing Makers Network exists to explore the balance between technology, art, and culture by bringing together like minded individuals and groups in an environment that supports our common passions for technology, art and culture. We seek to inspire the people of the greater Lansing area to become a community of makers.

The official LMN website is coming soon, but in the meantime, here are some temporary links to information about LMN. Join the Google Group to keep up to date with what LMN is up to.
